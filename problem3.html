3.Soil Health and Fertility

Solution:

a.Crop Rotation and Cover Crops:

Rotating paddy crops with legumes or other non-rice crops can improve soil fertility.Legumes like soybeans or cowpeas can fix nitrogen in the soil, enhancing its nutrient content.Cover crops prevent soil erosion,suppress weeds, and add organic matter when they decompose.

b.Soil Testing:

Regular soil testing helps in understanding the nutrient composition and pH levels of the soil.Based on these results, farmers can apply fertilizers and soil amendments more precisely, avoiding over-application or deficiencies.

c.Biofertilizers and Microbial Inoculants:

Using microbial inoculants such as mycorrhizae or rhizobium helps in enhancing nutrient uptake by plants.These biofertilizers aid in improving soil fertility naturally.
